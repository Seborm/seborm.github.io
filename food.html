Need to cut down on carbs?

I love bread, pasta, and any and all carbs. I am a true believer that we all need to treat ourselves, but when you are feeling carbo-bloated try replacing pasta with spaghetti squash. I know this sounds scary and weird, but try it! As a pasta lover I will say it is different, but it can also be a better choice sometimes!

Easy breakfast idea

I love avocados. They are the best (or I think so). When you are in a bind and running late, mash up some avo on toast and add salt and pepper!
