The Fabulous View From Under 5'2"

What is on my mind??

Here are some of my BIG ideas! Let me know what you think!

What has been popular?

This fall 70's fashion is very popular! Many stores are featuring high waisted jeans, flared pants, and buttoned skirts! I personally really like this trend! Fashion is always changing, so it is exciting to see that todays trends are being inspired by the past!

Easy breakfast idea

I love avacados. They are the best (or I think so). When you are in a bind and running late, mash up some avo on toast and add salt and pepper!

Lipstick

I think that a bold lip color can totally spice up a look! Do not be afraid to try new colors out! Stores like Colourpop sell lipsticks as low as $5, so why not?!
